Crispy Roast Potatoes

Wow your guests with these super crispy roast potatoes.

  1. Peel and chop your potatoes
  2. Add to boiling water and boil for 20 minutes
  3. Drain potatoes and allow them to cool slightly so they harden
  4. Toss in lots of oil
  5. Add potatoes to a hot oven for 1 hour
  6. Remove pototoes and add to the air fryer for ultra crispyness
  7. Season with salt and serve

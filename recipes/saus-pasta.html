Sausage Pasta

A quick and easy sausage pasta dish with, a tasty homemade tomoto sauce topped with cheddar cheese.

  1. Chop 1 onion and add to a hot pan of oil
  2. Add garlic to the pan
  3. Boil water and add pasta
  4. Fry sausages
  5. Once onions and garlic have softened add tomatoes
  6. Season sauce with salt,pepper and italian spice mix, leave to simmer
  7. Mix the cooked pasta, sauce and sausages
  8. Top with cheddar cheese.

Hot Wings

Crispy skin chicken wings coated in hot sauce

  1. Prepare wings by cutting in half through the joint
  2. Create spice mix using chilli powder, smoked paprika, garlic powder, ginger, onion powder and salt
  3. Toss wings in spice mix, then add to air fryer and cook for 20 minutes
  4. Mix your favourite hot sauce with some melted butter and then toss the ings in once cooked
  5. Eat and enjoy!
